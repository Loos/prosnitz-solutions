Joseph Prosnitz

Joseph Prosnitz the entrepreneur behind Up-Ride has Crohn's disease and related Juvenile Arthritis (Ankylosing Spondylitis). Joseph does marathon and long distance triathlon to stay active. He developed Up-Ride to make run training less painful and more convenient during Chicago winters. Joseph and the Up-Ride team believe that people, especially the physically challenged (injury or chronic illness), deserve the health and happiness that exercise provides at a price they can afford.
