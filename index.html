Up-Ride is a bicycle accessory kit that turns a normal bicycle into a stationary elliptical exercise machine. Up-Ride gives you a gym quality elliptical running experience in the comfort of your home without the pain of traditional running. Up-Ride is exercise for when you don't have the time or desire to go to the gym or run outside. Up-Ride is a patent pending platform technology for low impact cardio fitness with the elliptical conversion being the proof of concept.
